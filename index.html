Privacy Policy

Effective July 16, 2026 · Applies to Marquee for macOS

Marquee collects nothing. Everything it does happens on your Mac.

Data collection

Marquee does not collect, transmit, store, or sell any personal data. There are no analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting services, no advertising identifiers, no accounts, and no tracking of any kind.

Your content

Screenshots, screen recordings, project files, headlines, and translations are processed entirely on your Mac. Marquee only reads files you explicitly choose, and writes only where you tell it to. AI-assisted headlines and translations run on Apple's on-device models — your content is never sent to Marquee or to any third party.

App Store Connect uploads

If you connect an App Store Connect API key, it is stored exclusively in your Mac's Keychain and never leaves your machine. When you choose to upload, Marquee communicates directly with Apple's App Store Connect API over an encrypted connection using short-lived tokens signed on your Mac. No intermediary server exists; we could not see your uploads even if we wanted to.

What we could see

Nothing. Marquee operates no servers and receives no data from the app.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Because the app is built around on-device processing, we do not expect it to change in substance.
