Questions

Does Marquee upload to the App Store for me?

Yes. Connect your own App Store Connect API key — stored only in your Mac's Keychain — and Marquee pushes your screenshots straight into your listing's draft. You still review and submit for review yourself.

Is my unreleased app safe?

Yes. Screenshot analysis and copy drafting run on your Mac with Apple's on-device models. Nothing is sent to us — we run no servers. Only when you choose to publish does Marquee send your files to Apple's App Store Connect, over an encrypted connection.

Does it guarantee my listing passes review?

No — and we won't pretend otherwise. Marquee preflights every screenshot against Apple's pixel spec (size, opacity, color space) before it's written, which removes a common class of asset rejections. App Store review itself is Apple's call, not ours.

Do I need Apple Intelligence?

Not for the core. Importing, designing, exact-pixel export, and the ASO audit all work without it. On-device headline drafting and translation need Apple Intelligence available on your Mac, with a clear fallback when it isn't.

What does it cost?

Free to try. A one-time unlock enables editing, export, and publishing — no subscription. Launch pricing is shown on the Mac App Store at release.

What do I need to run it?

macOS 26 or later. Marquee prepares listing assets for iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, Apple TV, and Apple Vision Pro.
